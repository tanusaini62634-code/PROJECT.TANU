Our Story

A Legacy of
Authentic Flavors

Started in 1985 by Sharma Ji in the heart of Old Delhi, our coffee house has been serving the finest chai and coffee for over three decades. We source our tea leaves from Assam and Darjeeling, and our coffee beans from the misty hills of Coorg.

Every cup is prepared with the same love and dedication that our founder instilled in us. Our secret masala blend, passed down through generations, makes our chai truly special.
